# Artificial Intelligence | Reinforcement Learning | LLM Integration | Strategic & Social Science Analytics

This profile represents an integrated research and engineering framework combining **Reinforcement Learning (RL)**, **Large Language Models (LLMs)**, **strategic AI analysis**, and **quantitative & mixed-method social science analytics**, grounded in **academic research discipline** and **national R&D project standards (TEKNOFEST & TÜBİTAK)**.

## Academic & Strategic Orientation

The work represented here follows a dual-layer structure:

- **Academic Layer**  
  Emphasis on mathematical foundations, learning theory, statistical modeling, convergence behavior, interpretability, and scientific validity.

- **Strategic Engineering Layer**  
  Emphasis on scalable deployment, performance accountability, system robustness, social impact assessment, and strategic decision intelligence.

The central aim is to establish **measurable, explainable, statistically grounded, and operationally reliable intelligent systems** across both technical and social domains.
